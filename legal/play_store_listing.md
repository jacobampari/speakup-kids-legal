## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
SpeakUp Kids is a daily speaking-confidence practice for children ages 5 to 14. Kids speak to a friendly mascot on camera, follow a short teleprompter or cue card, and earn coins, streaks, and belts — Yellow up to Black — by showing up every day.

Built for kids who freeze when it's their turn to speak. Built for parents who want a habit, not a one-shot lesson. Built for teachers who want a classroom rotation tool that takes one tap.

🎤 EVERY DAY, ONE MISSION
A new speaking topic appears every morning, tuned to your child's age and current belt level. No browsing 100 topics — just today's one, ready to go.

🥋 BELTS, NOT LEADERBOARDS
Six belt levels (White → Yellow → Orange → Green → Blue → Purple → Black). Each belt unlocks fresh topics. Belt-ups celebrate every kid, every time. There are no public scores, no comparisons to other children, no winners and losers. Belts measure "you showed up," not "you were the best."

🦊 LUMI, BEEP, AND PIP
Pick a mascot — the fox, the robot, or the turtle — who sits with your child on every screen so they never feel alone. The mascot reads the prompt aloud, claps at the end, and never judges.

📜 TELEPROMPTER OR CUE CARDS
Younger kids can read along to a scrolling teleprompter (font size adjustable in case of dyslexia or low vision). Older kids can switch to cue cards and speak in their own words. Both modes work for every topic.

✨ MAKE YOUR OWN TOPIC
Type a noun — "my dog," "my future job," "the ocean" — and the app generates a kid-safe speaking script in one of five pedagogical stages.

💪 BUILT FOR EMOTIONAL SAFETY
- No public profiles, no chat, no leaderboards, no AI "scoring" of kids
- No timers during recording — anxiety-free
- Recordings stay on the device unless an adult chooses to share
- COPPA + GDPR-K + PDPA compliant

👨‍👩‍👧 PARENTS / MENTORS DASHBOARD
Sign in to track your child's streak, mission completion rate, and weekly insights. Set a daily reminder. Switch between siblings — each kid has their own profile, streak, and belt.

🏫 FOR PRESCHOOLS AND CLASSROOMS
A teacher pins a daily mission for the class, then taps any student to switch the device into that kid's profile and hand it over. One tap per kid. The kid practises the teacher-pinned topic, the session syncs back, the teacher rotates to the next kid. The actual video stays on the device.

🎁 GENEROUS FREE TIER
The free version includes ALL 68+ topics, all belts up to Black, unlimited recordings, all gamification — for one kid. A small watermark appears on saved recordings; a single non-personalised ad shows when a parent taps Share. Premium removes both (RM 8.80 monthly, or RM 89 yearly for up to 4 kids). Schools subscribe per-student with founding-member pricing.

🔒 AD AND DATA POLICY
Inside the kid's flow there are zero ads. No ads on the home screen, no ads during recording, no ads in celebrations, anywhere a child taps. The only ad placement is at parent-initiated share time — adult sees an ad, kid never does. Ads run in Google AdMob's Tag-For-Families mode (non-personalised, G-rated only). We do not run any other tracking SDK.

Made in Malaysia by a parent who got tired of school plays where his kid couldn't get the words out. Built for kids who'd rather hide than speak — and want a way to practise where nobody is watching except a fox who only ever claps.
```

## What to NOT write

- Don't use the words "best," "winner," "top-rated," or any superlative — Play scans these and flags listings as "misleading" when there's no evidence
- Don't promise AI feedback or "scoring of clarity, pace, eye contact" — we don't ship that yet
- Don't say "exclusive content" if every topic is available in the free tier
- Don't mention competitor names (Khan Kids, Lingokids, etc.) — Play rejects competitor-tagged listings
- Don't claim COPPA "certified" — say "compliant" or "designed for"; certification is a separate paid programme
